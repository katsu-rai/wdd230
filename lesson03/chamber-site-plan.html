Site Plan

Site Name: Kenko Rice Co., Ltd. | Tasty and Safe rice

Audience

Site Purpose

To show who make the rice and products and what is the process of growing rice so that customers would feel safe to purchase them. Another purpose is to make it easy for customers to reach out to the company.

Scenarios

  • A mother is planning to buy rice for her children but he does not know which one is safe to eat. This website provides who grow the rice and the information about process of making rice.
  • An old guy heard about this rice company but he does not know where to find the contact information. This website provides the contact informaiton.
  • A hotel owner is looking for rice for their buffet. He wants to see the rice that have won awards before. This website provides the history of awards.
