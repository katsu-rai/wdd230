Hick's Law

Apple

https://www.apple.com/

This is the example of Hick’s Law. The website is designed to lead customers to make actions for either buy or learn more about the product. The website is very sophisticated.

Visual Hierarchy

The New York Times

https://www.nytimes.com/

This is the example of Visual Hierarchy. The company’s name is the biggest and hot news follow. As you scroll, you will see more news but they are displayed smaller compared to the hot news at the top. They change the size of font and image depending on how interesting they are to readers.

Rule of Thirds

FPP

https://fpp.net/work

This is the example of Rule of Thirds. The picture on the website is clearly designed based on the Rule of Thirds. If you draw the guidelines in your mind, you would know how well the website is organized.
